Travel

By car: Dunster is approximately 3hrs 30mins from London. Limited car parking will be available at the Tithe Barn on the day of the wedding. Public parking is also available within the village for £4.80 per day at Dunster Steep and Park Street car parks.

By rail: The nearest National Rail station is Taunton, which is approximately 1hr 45mins from London Paddington. From Taunton, the number 28 or 18 bus will connect you to Dunster, which takes a further 1hr 20mins. Please see Great Western Railway for details.

What to do

Dunster is situated in the sweeping hills of the Exmoor National Park. Dunster has a wealth of historic buildings, from the National Trust Dunster Castle to the Old Yarn Market in the middle of the High Street.

There are also lots of beautiful walking trails to help cure your hangover; we recommend the walk to the top of Conygar Tower, which is the folly you see from afar as you approach Dunster.

Dunster beach and train station (served by steam trains) are walkable from the village and worth a visit.
